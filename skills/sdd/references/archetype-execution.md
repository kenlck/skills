# Archetype execution

Read only the section matching the issue's primary archetype. The [shared work-package contract](../../to-seo-issues/references/work-package-contract.md) remains authoritative for archetype definitions and controllable completion; this guide supplies execution technique.

## Baseline/setup

Inspect the named source and record its current observable state before changing it. Establish only the inventory, access, instrumentation, or baseline specified by acceptance, then query the same observable interface to verify the resulting state. Preserve the query, timestamp, scope, and any sampling or coverage limitation.

## Evidence/brief

Turn the deliverable and its downstream decision into explicit research questions. Gather evidence from the named sources, maintain a source ledger with dates and limitations, distinguish observations from assumptions, and synthesize only what the next executor needs. Verify every material claim against its cited source and map the final brief to its acceptance rubric.

## Production

Produce against the specification oracle one criterion at a time. After each slice, inspect the resulting artifact through its intended audience and delivery format, run applicable validators, and map the relevant passage or field to the criterion it satisfies. Preserve factual support, validator output, and unresolved limitations with the artifact.

## Publication/change

Capture the named surface's prior observable state, apply the exact authorized change, and inspect the resulting state through the surface its audience uses. Use the final content, URL or identifier, visibility, and timestamp as publication evidence; a tool's success response establishes only that the tool call returned.

## Outreach cycle

Resolve the approved finite batch and assign a stable identifier to each target. Before each external action, verify the sender, recipient, approved message bounds, channel, and prior activity. Record the attempted or confirmed action and timestamp in the required activity log. Treat replies, placements, and links as later outcomes.

## Outcome review

Verify the observation window against current time before collecting evidence. Snapshot the measurement contract's named sources with dates, definitions, and coverage; keep its baseline, indicators, guardrails, and decision rules fixed throughout the review. Return the supported continue, adjust, or stop decision together with missing data, attribution limits, and any follow-up recommendation.
